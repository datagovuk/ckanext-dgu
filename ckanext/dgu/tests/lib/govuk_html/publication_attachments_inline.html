Commodity prices

Prices for selected agricultural and horticultural produce.

Prices for selected agricultural and horticultural produce are published on a weekly or monthly basis in the following spreadsheets. The data source depends on the item but includes prices taken from trade journals or other organisations in addition to prices collected by the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (Defra).

Changes to the Eggs and Poultry (wholesale) prices from April 2014. Minor amendments have been made to the categories of data collected. Most noticeable is that frozen chicken now reports imported prices rather than home-grown.

  • Animal feed (straights) - monthly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 1.13MB)
  • Bananas (wholesale prices) - weekly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 2.67MB)
  • Cattle compensation prices - monthly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 123KB)
  • Eggs and poultry (wholesale prices) - weekly (ODS, 125KB)
  • Hay and straw - monthly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 298KB)
  • Livestock (store stock, England & Wales) - monthly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 303KB)
  • Other animal feedingstuffs - monthly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 1.13MB)
  • Price series for cereals - weekly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 302KB)
  • Price series for poultry, eggs, butter, cheese, potatoes and sugar - weekly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 429KB)
  • Price series for finished cattle, sheep and pigs - weekly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 262KB)
  • Quantities sold and price of cereals (England & Wales) - weekly (MS Excel Spreadsheet, 547KB)

For further information please contact:
prices@defra.gsi.gov.uk
Twitter: @DefraStats
Defra Helpline: 03459 33 55 77 (Monday to Friday: 8am to 6pm)
